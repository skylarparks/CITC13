Nine Tail Fox

The nine-tailed fox is a mythical creature that has been a part of Japanese, Chinese, and Korean folklore for centuries. In these cultures, the nine-tailed fox is often depicted as a shape-shifting creature that can take on human form and has the ability to control the elements.

different types of foxes

There are many different types of foxes in the world, but the nine-tailed fox is one of the most famous and well-known. In Japanese folklore, the nine-tailed fox is known as the "Kitsune," while in Chinese folklore, it is known as the "Huli Jing." In Korean folklore, the nine-tailed fox is known as the "Gumiho."
